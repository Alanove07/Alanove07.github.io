About Me

Creative Professional from Kerala

Welcome to my digital space! I'm Alanove Jenny Bazil, a passionate creative professional from Kozhikode, Kerala, India. I founded Nexa as my creative brand that encompasses art, design, and technology.

My journey spans from traditional portrait painting and acrylic art to modern web development and UI/UX design. I believe in bringing creativity and technology together to create meaningful experiences. My aspiration is to become a Commercial/Air Force Pilot while continuing my creative endeavors.

Through Nexa Art, I create portraits, logos, and digital art. Nexa Custom Forge handles PC building and hardware services, while Nexa Frames provides frame and photo print services.
